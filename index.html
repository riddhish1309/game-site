Tomb Raider is an action-adventure video game developed by Crystal Dynamics and published by Square Enix.Tomb Raider was first released on 5 March 2013 Crystal Dynamics began development of Tomb Raider soon after the release of Tomb Raider: Underworld in 2008. Rather than a sequel, the team decided to completely reboot the series, re-establishing the origins of Lara Croft for the second time, as they did with Tomb Raider: Legend.
